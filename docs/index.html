Our Pies.

Simply Heaven.

We create soul restoring baked goods.

Talk to us today!

As a young child I often helped my family in the kitchen.

Ever since, we’ve been working to make baked goods and pies better for everyone.

Here’s how we got started…

One thing I love about baking is that it always feels a little bit indulgent, a little bit unnecessary. I don't have to bake anything, but when I do, it's because I want to devote my time to making myself or someone else happy.

Simply making that decision feels like a gift to myself. Baking is a communal experience. Usually we don’t make a whole cake or batch of cookies just for ourselves. Instead, we tend to embark on baking with the intention to share what we’re making. We share with coworkers, with friends, or with someone who’s simply having a bad day... pretty much anyone who will indulge our compulsion or need to make treats and give them to people. Knowing that what you’re making will make other people happy is one of the warmest, fuzziest feelings there is. The fact that you get to eat some, too, is simply icing on the cake.

This is why I opened Perfect Pies! To bring my love to those around me in our community. Come on in. I guarantee you'll love every sight, smell, and taste.
